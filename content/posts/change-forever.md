title: 'Change Forever'
tags: ['poems']

Oh I start so much  
But I never compete  
My interests are running wild  
Like a Viking fleet

One day I wanna build something  
That makes the doubters hush  
Next day it falls to pieces  
And I'm the one left to sweep

I wanted to write a novel  
To make the ladies blush  
The whole story was so cheesy  
It made me look like a creep

Oh I tried making videos  
Then sent my stories down with a flush  
Nobody cared to buy them  
Even though I sold them dirt cheap

Wrestling was a dream  
I chased without a rush  
I might have even made it  
If my health hadn't sunk so deep

Cricket was in my bones  
Always swinging hard, never needed a push  
"Be practical" bled the love out slow  
I crumbled under the textbook heap

Now I'm writing crappy songs  
But I think I can crush  
I plan to write about my life  
Don't want to just sit here and weep

At some point I grew up, found lust for money and love for code  
Hustling my way out of the pit, chipping at the walls when bored  
It wasn't easy, wasn't always fun making my eyes bleed  
But I wanted something for myself, I was done marching with the horde

I lost my way many times, many times felt the coffin being lowered  
But I never rested, couldn't sleep, there was too much I couldn't afford  
Ain't going away, I'm gonna find my way, I'm too tough a breed  
I'm gonna fight and shout until I'm too loud to be ignored
