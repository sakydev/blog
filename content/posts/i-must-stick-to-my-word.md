Talk is cheap. It is meaningless unless backed by actions.

How many times have I failed to execute an action that I had promised myself to?

How many times have I knowingly wasted my time even though that's the biggest thing I preach to others?

How many times have I delayed a task or completely skipped it knowing that it was going to benefit me if I push through that face?

How many times do I tell myself to stick to a habit only to abandon it barely a week later? This has got to stop. I want to leave an impact behind. I want to leave a legacy that impacts people, that impacts my kids and generations to come.

I don't see it happening if I continue to waste my minutes like they don't mean nothing.

Every fucking second counts. I should also start removing emotions. They need to be removed when something tough needs to be done. I must not allow my thoughts to get into the play when I know something is worth doing for sure.

I must not allow the seeds of doubt to flourish inside of my hand and keep me from working on something that will impact me in the future.

I must respect my time.

Who is going to if I don't?
